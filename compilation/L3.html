Questions

Who can attend?

You may attend if:

  • You are 16 or older
  • You have previously completed a Signature Leadership Course, or
  • Are concurrently taking a Signature Leadership Course during the spring semester.

Who can earn the certification?

The certification is open to anyone who has completed all three Leadership Courses. To register for the third course, you need to have taken (or be taking) at least one Leadership Course.

When should I plan to arrive?

For those flying We will be conducting airport runs throughout the arrival days on Tuesday and Friday. Before the event, please plan to arrive by 3 PM on Tuesday so we can get you to the lodge in time for dinner and the evening opening. After the event, please choose flights that depart from Denver (DIA) after 1 PM on Friday.

For those driving If you're driving, please plan to arrive before 5 PM on Tuesday so you can get checked in.
