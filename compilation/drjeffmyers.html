Dr. Jeff Myers

If you haven't heard Dr. Jeff Myers, you are missing out! When you sign up for a Signature Leadership Course, Dr. Myers's energy and passion will keep you engaged as you learn how to turn your goals into action. You will laugh, ponder, and learn from his stories and life experiences as he explains how he has applied the concepts he presents in the Signature Leadership Series.

The Signature Leadership Courses are an exclusive opportunity to spend in-depth time learning from this trusted instructor. You'll read books that Dr. Myers has personally selected for the course and he'll invite you to listen to exclusive interviews he's conducted with friends like Chuck Colson, David Barton, and Voddie Baucham. Don't miss this unique opportunity to invest in your future as a leader!

Dr. Jeff Myers:

  • Has spent thousands of hours over twenty years teaching and mentoring teenagers and young adults.
  • Earned his Ph.D. in Communications from the University of Denver.
  • Taught leadership and MBA classes at Bryan College for 14 years.
  • Started the Get Ready to Lead e-mail newsletter with 13,000 subscribers.
  • Serves as president of Summit Ministries, a worldview and leadership training program for young adults.
  • Developed and wrote curricula and books on worldview, communication, and leadership.
  • Founder of the non-profits Passing the Baton and The Cultivate Project.
  • Lives in Colorado with his wife Danielle and their four children — Graham, Emma, Cate, and Stuart.
